# ST-510-Probability-and-Distribution-Theory
Course materials for ST 510 (Probability &amp; Distribution Theory): 

The course will cover essential concepts of probability and distribution
theory that are important for statistical inference. The material will be based
on the first five chapters from the text including: probability spaces, independence and
conditional probability, random variables, joint and conditional distributions, conditional
expectation, modeling dependence, transformations, quantiles, order statistics, modes of
convergence for sequences of random variables, basic inequalities, laws of small and large
numbers, the Central Limit Theorem and the multivariate Normal distribution.
